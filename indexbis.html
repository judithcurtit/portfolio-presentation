Hi, I'm Judith

Life Path

I was born and raised in France. I moved to Shanghai in 2003, where I met my now husband. We then moved to Bangladesh for almost 5 years. Enough time to have 2 daugthers. We arrived in Hong Kong in 2012 and we are really enjoying the city!

Hobbies

I did a lot of yoga while in Bangladesh. Now, I am more into hiking and running. Hong Kong is the perfect place so I am like a fish in the ocean! Being french, I like cooking, eating, having a glass of wine....

Moreover...

I have no experience whatsoever with coding so I am excited to start this new journey. I know it is a big challenge. I am not even sure, I realize what I am up to but anyway I am here to learn and I will do my best. I am looking forward to learn with and from you!
